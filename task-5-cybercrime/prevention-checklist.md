# Stay Safe Online - Checklist

## Prevention Tips
- Always verify the sender before clicking on any email or message link  
- Do not share personal information like passwords, OTPs, or bank details with anyone  
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts  
- Never approve unknown UPI payment requests without checking details  
- Do not scan random QR codes, especially for receiving money  
- Always double-check website URLs before entering sensitive information  

## Report Cybercrime
