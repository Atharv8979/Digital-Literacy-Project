# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- Think before posting anything online  
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content  
- Share useful and positive information  
- Be respectful while commenting or messaging others  
- Keep your profile clean and professional  
- Verify information before sharing it online  

## Don’ts
- Don’t share personal information like phone number or address  
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information  
- Don’t post anything offensive or harmful  
- Don’t click on suspicious links or messages  
- Don’t misuse social media during important work or study time  
- Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people
